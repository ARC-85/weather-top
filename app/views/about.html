About Weather Top Release 1

Weather Top Release 1 gives you a comprehensive overview of your weather station data. In this version, users can view their weather stations from the Dashboard, with the ability to view specific weather data for each station, including:
  • Weather type
  • Temperature (Celsius and Fahrenheit)
  • Wind Behaviour (Strength, Direction, Wind Chill)
  • Pressure
Users can add new readings for each station (via each Station page), or add new stations (via the Dashboard).
