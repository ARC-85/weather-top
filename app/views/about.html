About Weather Top Release 2

Weather Top Release 2 gives you a comprehensive overview of your weather station data. In this version, users can setup a personalised login, allowing them to view their individual weather stations from the Dashboard. This includes the ability to view specific weather data for each station, including:
  • Weather type (including helpful demo icons)
  • Temperature (Celsius (min and max) and Fahrenheit)
  • Wind Behaviour (Strength (min and max), Direction, Wind Chill)
  • Pressure (min and max)
Users can add new readings for each station (via each Station page), or add new stations (via the Dashboard).
